George Brown College, shortly known as GBC, is a publicly funded and fully accredited college of applied arts and technology with three campuses in downtown Toronto, Ontario. GBC grants diplomas in a wide variety of full-time programs, including apprenticeships, certificates, and part-time and continuing education programs. It comprises a total of three campuses: Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront. It is well-recognized for its excellent academic programs in business, arts, health sciences, engineering technology, and hospitality management.}
